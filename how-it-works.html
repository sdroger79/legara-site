The Legara Model

A workforce platform purpose-built for FQHCs.

Not staffing. Not telehealth. A complete ecosystem — platform, operations, and financial infrastructure — designed for the compliance requirements, operational realities, and mission imperatives of safety-net healthcare.

The Platform
Three layers working together.

Legara operates as an integrated ecosystem with three core functions — each designed to remove friction and create value for both health centers and clinicians.

01

Workforce Network

Legara sources, credentials, and maintains a network of independent behavioral health clinicians — CA-licensed, experienced in FQHC environments, and ready to integrate. Therapy (LCSW/LMFT/Psychologist) and psychiatry (PMHNP) coverage available with bilingual and culturally competent providers.

02

Operational Infrastructure

Our operations layer handles the work that typically buries health center staff: scheduling coordination, provider onboarding, documentation workflows, EHR integration, compliance management, and ongoing performance support.

03

Financial Architecture

Encounter-based payment processing, clinician compensation management, and transparent reporting. Your costs align directly with patient revenue — no salaries, no benefits overhead, no staffing risk.

Deployment Process
From first conversation to full productivity.

Every Legara deployment follows a structured process designed to get clinicians seeing patients as quickly as the FQHC credentialing process allows. Here's what the typical timeline looks like.

Weeks 1–4

Discovery & Alignment

We assess your health center's behavioral health needs, patient volume, payer mix, and operational workflows. We identify the right provider configuration — specialty, language, and schedule.

Weeks 4–12

Contracting & Credentialing

We execute the partnership agreement and begin the credentialing process with your health center. This phase is largely driven by FQHC-side timelines — we manage it proactively.

Weeks 8–16

Onboarding & Integration

Clinicians are onboarded to your EHR, scheduling system, and documentation workflows. We handle the administrative setup so your staff doesn't carry the burden.

Weeks 12–20

Panel Build & Ramp

Clinicians begin seeing patients and building their panels. Full panel productivity is typically achieved within 4–6 weeks of first patient contact. You don't pay until encounters generate revenue.

Ongoing

Sustained Operations

Once deployed, Legara provides ongoing operational support: scheduling management, compliance monitoring, performance reporting, and the ability to scale coverage up or down as your needs evolve.

Seamless Integration
Fits your existing workflows. Doesn't replace them.

Legara clinicians operate within your health center's existing systems — not outside them. The integration is designed to feel like an extension of your team, not an external vendor.

💻

EHR Integration

Clinicians document directly in your EHR system. No parallel workflows, no data migration, no duplicate records. We support all major FQHC EHR platforms.

📅

Scheduling Coordination

Legara manages scheduling within your existing systems. Patients are scheduled the same way they would be with employed staff — no separate booking process.

📋

Compliance Management

We maintain credentialing, licensing verification, and compliance documentation — structured so that adding Legara capacity preserves your existing compliance posture.

📈

Encounter Reporting

Transparent reporting on encounter volumes, clinician productivity, and financial performance. You always know exactly what's happening and what it costs.

👥

Care Coordination

Legara clinicians participate in your care coordination workflows — warm handoffs, treatment planning, and multidisciplinary team integration.

⇵

Flexible Scaling

Add coverage for growing demand or reduce during slower periods. Scale from part-time to full-time per specialty without the commitment or risk of a new hire.
