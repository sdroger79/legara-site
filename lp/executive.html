The Structural Challenge

Why Hiring Alone Doesn't Solve It

Behavioral health demand at FQHCs has grown 54% since 2018. The operating model it runs inside has not changed. Shared scheduling, generalist administrative support, 6 to 9 months from hire to a full caseload, 30% annual turnover. Each new hire restarts the cycle. The constraint is not effort or budget. It is structural.
