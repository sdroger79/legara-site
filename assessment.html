How Strong Is Your Behavioral Health Infrastructure?

Most FQHCs have invested heavily in behavioral health. Fewer have built the operational layer that makes those investments productive.

The reality

Behavioral health visits at FQHCs have grown over 50% since 2018, but the infrastructure hasn't kept pace. Scheduling is shared with primary care. No-shows consume 20-30% of capacity with no backfill process. New providers take 9+ months to reach a full panel.

77% of health centers report mental health provider shortages, with wait times stretching to 20 weeks.
What this measures

This assessment scores the 7 operational dimensions that determine whether your BH program can scale. Not just headcount, but the structural factors underneath: access, utilization, retention, and scheduling infrastructure.
