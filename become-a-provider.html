For Clinicians
Practice independently. Make a difference in the communities that need it most.

Legara connects independent behavioral health clinicians with FQHCs and safety-net healthcare organizations — with the operational support to let you focus on patients, not paperwork.

Why Legara
Your practice. Your autonomy. Our support.

Legara is built on a simple principle: clinicians do their best work when they have the freedom to practice independently and the infrastructure to handle everything else. Here's what that looks like.

⚖

Clinical Independence

You maintain full clinical autonomy. Legara's model is built around your independence — not employment. You make the clinical decisions. We handle the operational side.

📅

Flexible Scheduling

Work part-time or full-time. Set your availability. Scale your commitment as your schedule allows. Legara matches opportunities to your preferences — not the other way around.

📋

Administrative Support

Scheduling coordination, onboarding, documentation workflows, compliance support, and EHR integration — all managed by Legara's operations team so you can focus on patient care.

💖

Mission-Driven Work

Every patient you see through Legara is at an FQHC or safety-net health center — organizations serving underserved communities with the greatest need for behavioral health access.

💰

Reliable Compensation

Transparent, encounter-based compensation. You see patients, we handle the financial processing. No chasing payments, no billing complexity, no surprises.

👥

Professional Community

Join a network of behavioral health professionals committed to expanding access. Collaborate with FQHC care teams while maintaining your independent practice.

Who We Work With
Licensed behavioral health clinicians who want to make an impact.

Legara works with independent behavioral health clinicians — therapists (LCSW/LMFT/Psychologist) and psychiatric providers (PMHNP/Psychiatrist) — who are licensed in California and interested in serving FQHC patient populations. We particularly value clinicians who bring bilingual capacity and cultural competence to their practice.

Whether you're looking to fill part of your week with meaningful work or build your entire schedule around FQHC patients, Legara can match opportunities to your availability and interests.

What we look for
Active California license
LCSW, LMFT, Psychologist (PhD/PsyD), PMHNP, or Psychiatrist (MD/DO)
FQHC or community health experience
Preferred but not required — we'll support your onboarding
Telehealth capability
Most Legara deployments are telehealth-based
Bilingual capacity (valued)
Spanish-English bilingual clinicians are in high demand
Commitment to underserved populations
Our patients are the ones who need you most
How It Works
From interest to seeing patients.

The process is straightforward. We'll learn about your background, match you with the right opportunity, and handle the operational setup so you can start making an impact.

Begin serving FQHC patients with full operational support. Legara manages scheduling, compliance, and financial processing — you focus on care.
