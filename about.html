Our Story
Decades in the field. A platform built to fix it.

Legara didn't start as a technology company. It started with a team that had spent years inside the behavioral health system — running programs, managing operations, overseeing finances, and watching the same problems compound across every safety-net healthcare organization we worked with.

Clinician shortages. Unsustainable hiring cycles. Wait times measured in months. And a traditional employment model that put all the financial risk on health centers that could least afford it.

We designed Legara from the ground up to solve those problems structurally: a workforce platform that connects health centers with independent clinicians, an operational infrastructure that removes friction for both sides, and a financial architecture that aligns everyone's incentives around patient encounters and expanded access to care.
