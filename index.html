The Challenge
Your patients can't wait 18 weeks for care.

FQHCs face a structural crisis in behavioral health. Clinician shortages and the high cost of full-time employment leave health centers with shrinking capacity and growing wait lists — while the communities you serve fall further behind. The traditional answer comes with salary obligations, administrative burden, and months of lead time you don't have.

Your employed team is the backbone. Legara extends their reach — adding vetted clinicians who integrate into your workflows, with costs that align to revenue and capacity that scales without another hiring cycle.

The Legara Model
Flexible capacity. No FTE risk. Rapid deployment.

Legara is a behavioral health workforce platform purpose-built for FQHCs. We connect your health center with a vetted network of independent clinicians — integrated seamlessly into your existing scheduling, EHR, and care coordination workflows. You pay only for completed patient encounters.

Why Legara
Not a staffing agency. Not telehealth. Something built specifically for you.

Most behavioral health vendors were designed for a different market. Legara is different — built as an ecosystem with the compliance architecture, operational infrastructure, and financial model that FQHCs actually need.

Aligned Incentives, Higher Utilization

Employed BH providers average ~52% effective utilization — between meetings, trainings, and a 25% no-show rate, barely half their paid time produces a billable visit. Legara clinicians run at ~82%. No organizational overhead. Incentivized scheduling. And you only pay for completed encounters.

Operational Infrastructure Included

Scheduling support, onboarding, compliance coordination, and EHR integration. Administrative lift stays low — for you and your team.

Rapid Deployment

Providers achieve full productivity within 4–6 weeks of onboarding. Faster access to care for your patients from day one.

Protects Your Compliance Posture

Clinician independence is preserved by design. Adding external behavioral health capacity through Legara doesn't create new regulatory exposure for your organization.
