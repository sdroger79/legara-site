The Legara Model
A workforce platform with operational infrastructure built in.

Legara is not a staffing agency that places a clinician and walks away. Every provider is supported by dedicated operational staff, every health center has a named relationship owner, and every encounter flows through a compliance architecture designed for FQHC regulatory requirements.

01

Clinician Network

Independent behavioral health providers sourced, vetted, and credentialed by Legara. CA-licensed, experienced in FQHC environments, matched to your patient population. Therapy and prescriber coverage, with bilingual and culturally competent providers available.

02

Operational Infrastructure

Every provider is backed by a dedicated Patient Service Representative handling scheduling, patient coordination, and documentation. Every health center has a Partner Relationship Owner as their single point of contact. This is why utilization is 82% and turnover is under 3%.

03

Encounter-Based Economics

You pay a flat rate per completed patient encounter. No salary, no benefits, no recruiter fees, no cost during the 5-month ramp period. Your health center retains full clinical authority, full billing control, and full compliance oversight.

Why Legara
Purpose-built operational infrastructure for FQHC behavioral health.

Most approaches to the behavioral health workforce shortage address one piece of the problem. Staffing agencies place a clinician. Telehealth companies connect a screen. Neither builds the operational layer that determines whether providers succeed and stay. Legara built that layer.

Dedicated Support for Every Provider

Each clinician is backed by a full-time Patient Service Representative handling no more than 3 to 4 providers. Scheduling, documentation, patient coordination. All handled.

A Named Relationship Owner for Your Health Center

Your Partner Relationship Owner serves 2 to 5 FQHCs and coordinates directly with your BH Director. One call, one person, one answer.

Compliance by Architecture, Not by Promise

Three-entity structure designed around California's corporate practice of medicine law. Your health center retains full clinical authority. Legara never exercises clinical control.

Economics That Align With Your Mission

Per-encounter pricing. Zero cost during the 5-month ramp. You pay only for completed patient encounters. No salary, no benefits, no FTE risk.
