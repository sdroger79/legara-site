Partner Profiles
9 health centers. No two deployments alike.

Every Legara partnership is structured around the health center's specific patient population, specialty needs, and operational capacity. Here are representative profiles from across the network. Details are anonymized to protect partner confidentiality. We're happy to arrange reference conversations directly.

Multi-Site FQHC, Southern California

12+ clinic sites | Large urban/suburban patient population

Deployment: 8 Legara providers (mix of therapy and prescriber)

Result: Reduced psychiatry wait from 18 weeks to under 2 weeks. Therapy wait from 10 weeks to under 3. Largest single health center deployment in the network.

This health center needed scale quickly across multiple locations. Legara deployed providers in phases, with a dedicated PRO managing the operational relationship across all sites and PSRs coordinating scheduling within each site's EHR.

Rural FQHC, Northern California

4 clinic sites | Rural and frontier communities

Deployment: 3 Legara providers (therapy focus)

Result: First behavioral health access in 2 of 4 sites where no in-person provider could be recruited. Panel at full productivity within 5 weeks of first patient.

Geography made in-person recruitment impossible for this health center's most remote sites. Telehealth-native delivery meant licensed providers in urban areas could serve patients in rural communities without relocation.

Mid-Size FQHC, Central California

6 clinic sites | Mixed urban and agricultural communities

Deployment: 4 Legara providers (therapy and prescriber)

Result: Added prescriber capacity that the health center had been unable to fill internally for over a year. Encounter volume ramped to target within 6 weeks.

The internal hiring process had stalled on a psychiatry position for 14 months. Legara placed a PMHNP with prescribing authority, backed by MD supervision, and the position was productive within weeks of credentialing completion.

Community Health Center, Coastal California

3 clinic sites | Diverse multilingual patient population

Deployment: 2 Legara providers (bilingual therapy)

Result: Launched Spanish-language behavioral health services that the health center had never been able to offer internally. No-show rate 12%, below network average.

Patient population was predominantly Spanish-speaking, but the health center had no bilingual behavioral health providers on staff. Legara matched bilingual LCSWs with specific cultural competency in the populations served.

How We Operate
The protocols behind the numbers.

These aren't aspirational standards. They are current operating procedures, refined over a decade of managing behavioral health deployments for California FQHCs.

Chart Note Completion

Every encounter documented and locked within 24 hours. No exceptions.

Nonclinical Spot-Checks

Biweekly reviews covering a minimum of 3% of all encounters or five per clinician, whichever is greater. Findings delivered to your Compliance Officer within ten banking days.

PSR Ratio

Each Patient Service Representative supports no more than 3 to 4 providers. This is capped, not averaged.

PRO Coverage

Each Partner Relationship Owner serves 2 to 5 health centers. They know your BH Director by name.

Provider Replacement

If a clinician leaves (under 3% annual turnover), Legara sources, credentials, and deploys a replacement. Your health center is never left without coverage.

Credentialing Accuracy

Legara assembles and submits complete credentialing packets. Internal FQHC approval and privileging timelines are the primary variable.
