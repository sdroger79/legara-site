Where Your Money Actually Goes (Internal Hire)
Assumptions: 32 scheduled clinical hrs/wk, 30 days PTO/sick/CME. Internal hire ramp: 3-month recruit, 3-week onboard, 3-month credentialing, 3-month payer enrollment (overlapping), 3-month caseload build. Encounter rate: 1.0/hr (therapy). Avg. encounter revenue: $230 (PPS + copays + secondaries). Legara rate: $155/encounter, all-in.* Support staff at 0.25 FTE (~$11K/yr). All figures are estimates — your results will vary.
*Rates shown reflect standard encounter rates by provider type. Final rates are confirmed during contracting and may vary based on licensure, encounter complexity, and agreement terms.
How we calculate this
Benefits & tax multiplier (1.4×) Bureau of Labor Statistics, Employer Costs for Employee Compensation (ECEC). Healthcare employer benefits average 29–38% of total compensation.
Provider turnover (30%) National Association of Community Health Centers (NACHC) workforce surveys; behavioral health turnover at FQHCs consistently ranges 25–35%.
Ramp timeline (6-9 months internal vs. as few as 6 weeks Legara) Based on standard credentialing and payer enrollment timelines reported by FQHC operations leaders. Legara's timeline is shorter because our providers are pre-credentialed with major payers.
Ramp productivity (25% → 65% → 100%) Graduated productivity during credential/payer enrollment (25%) and caseload build (65%) reflects typical panel-loading curves. Zero revenue during recruit and onboard phases.
Encounter revenue ($230/encounter) Prospective Payment System (PPS) reimbursement plus patient copays and secondary insurance. Varies by state and FQHC — adjust this input to match your actual rate.
Legara flat rate ($155–$165/encounter) All-inclusive: provider compensation, platform, and MSO servicing. No additional costs for benefits, recruitment, supervision, or support staff. Rate varies by provider type (therapy vs. prescriber).
Recruiter/placement fee ($8,000) Industry average for behavioral health provider recruitment in community health settings.
